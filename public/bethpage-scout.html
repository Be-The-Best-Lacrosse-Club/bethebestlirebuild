What to Watch For

1. Face-off X (First 30 sec of every quarter)

Cataldo is gone. Watch their new FOGO's technique — clamp, plunger, rake? Count wins vs losses. If they're under 55%, that's our edge.

2. Who Replaces Reinhardt (41G/26A)?

Their #1 scorer graduated. Someone is taking his shots now. Watch who gets the ball in scoring position — that's their dodger. Identify his hand, his dodge (split/roll/question mark), and where he shoots from.

3. Defensive Scheme

Are they running man with adjacent slide, crease slide, or a zone? Where do slides come from? How quickly? Gorman is 4th year — expect discipline.

4. Ride Package

Do they run a 10-man? Standard zone ride? Keep a safety? South Side has an elite middie in JJ Hawthorne — how did Bethpage handle him up top? Watch their LSM matchup.

5. End-of-Quarter & EMO

Late-quarter possessions and extra-man sets reveal their money plays. Write down their EMO formation (1-3-2? 3-3? 1-4-1?) and any repeated cuts.

Film Tip

If you're watching on phone, use YouTube's 0.5x or 0.75x speed for key possessions. Note timestamps of turnovers and transition goals — those are the breakdowns you want to exploit.

Tactical Read

Strengths & Weaknesses

Weaknesses — Attack These

1. Face-Off X

Lost Cataldo's 68% win rate. New FOGO has 4-7 games of varsity experience. Their offense can't score if they don't have the ball.

Action: Our best FOGO every draw. Aggressive wings. Count on 55%+ possession advantage.

2. Limited Offensive Firepower

7 goals vs LI Lutheran, 7 goals when they lost by 6. They're averaging 8.3/game — that includes scoreline-inflating wins. Against real defenses, they top out around 7-8.

Action: Keep it tight. If we're within 2 in the 4th quarter, we win — they don't have a go-to goal scorer.

3. Defense Under Pressure

April 8 loss: gave up 13 goals. A well-coached defense doesn't give up 13 unless someone is beating them consistently off the dodge or in transition.

Action: Push transition. Early offense before they set. If they are in their 6v6, dodge hard — make their sliders work.

Strengths — Respect These

1. Coaching Experience

Gorman in year 4 = no mental mistakes, no dumb penalties. They will be in the right spots at the right time.

Action: Don't count on easy looks. Earn every shot. Work through our progressions.

2. Senior Leadership

LILJ explicitly called out their experience and leadership. Translates to composure — they won't melt in big moments.

Action: Start fast. Build a lead early so they have to come out of their comfort zone to chase.

3. Home/Crowd Factor

Bethpage community is tight-knit — expect a real atmosphere if it's at their place.

Action: Score first. Silence the crowd. Don't let the game get emotional.

Plainedge Game Plan

How We Win

  1. Win the X — Own Possession

    Our starting FOGO every draw. Wings pressure their FOGO's exit. Target: 60% faceoff win rate. Every extra possession is 30+ seconds they don't have the ball.

  2. Push Transition Every Time

    On groundballs, faceoff wins, and saves — RUN. Their defense is disciplined but hasn't shown elite recovery. Numbers game: 4v3s and 5v4s are free goals.

  3. Pole Their #1 Dodger

    Once we identify from the Southside film who is replacing Reinhardt, our best LSM takes him every possession. Make him beat a long stick.

  4. Work the 2-Man From X

    Their defense relies on slides. Run our X 2-man to force their slide package. If they adjacent-slide, skip to the open. If they crease-slide, feed the crease.

  5. Don't Force Shots

    If it's not there, work the ball. They don't have the offense to beat us in a shootout — we don't need to beat them in one either. Possession first, shots second.

  6. Clear Clean

    Unknown if they ride hard. Expect standard 6-man zone. Adjacent pass, over the top when pressured. No risky middle passes in our own zone.

  7. Start Fast, Silence the Crowd

    First goal matters. If we score in the first 2 minutes, their senior leadership has to lean on experience instead of momentum. Take that away from them early.

Winning Score Target

Hold them to 7 goals. Score 10+. Their 2026 ceiling on offense is 8. Our job: be the real defense they struggled with on April 8.
