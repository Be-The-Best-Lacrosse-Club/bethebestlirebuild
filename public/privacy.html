Privacy Policy

Be The Best Lacrosse Club, LLC • Massapequa, NY

Who We Are

Be The Best (BTB) Lacrosse Club is a youth travel lacrosse organization based in Massapequa, New York. We operate boys and girls programs serving players from elementary school through high school. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information from our players, families, and staff.

Information We Collect

From Players & Parents

  • Player name, date of birth, graduation year, and jersey number
  • Parent/guardian name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Emergency contact information
  • Registration and payment history (processed through LeagueApps)
  • Event attendance and availability responses (through TeamSnap)

From Staff & Coaches

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment and compensation information (processed through Gusto)
  • Team and role assignments

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Social Security Numbers
  • Credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or financial credentials
  • Medical records or health information
  • Biometric data or facial recognition data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for club operations:

  • Team management, scheduling, and communication
  • Event coordination and availability tracking
  • Registration processing and billing through LeagueApps
  • Staff payroll processing through Gusto
  • Sending practice and game reminders via email or SMS
  • Internal operational reporting

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is shared only with the following service providers, solely for the purposes described above:

  • LeagueApps — registration and payment processing
  • TeamSnap — team management and scheduling
  • Gusto — staff payroll processing
  • Google Workspace — email communications
  • Twilio — SMS text message reminders
  • Airtable — operational data management
  • Netlify — application hosting

Each of these providers maintains their own privacy policies and security certifications. We select providers that demonstrate appropriate security practices including SOC 2 certification and/or PCI-DSS compliance.

How We Protect Your Information

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+)
  • API credentials and access tokens are stored as encrypted environment variables, never in source code
  • Our internal dashboard uses role-based access control limiting who can see what data
  • Financial data (payroll, payments) is restricted to the organization owner only
  • We do not store credit card or bank account information in our systems

Data Retention & Deletion

  • Active members: Data is retained for the duration of participation plus one calendar year
  • Staff/contractor records: Retained for 7 years per IRS requirements
  • Deletion requests: You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or your child
  • Correction — Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data from our systems
  • Opt-out — Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

BTB Lacrosse Club serves youth athletes. We collect player information (name, date of birth, graduation year) with the consent of a parent or guardian during the registration process. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent. All player accounts are managed through parent/guardian registrations on LeagueApps.

SMS & Email Communications

By registering with BTB Lacrosse Club, you consent to receive operational communications including:

  • Practice and game reminders
  • Schedule changes and cancellations
  • Payment reminders
  • Season updates and announcements

These communications are sent via email (from info@bethebestli.com) and SMS text messages. You may opt out of SMS reminders at any time by replying STOP to any text message or by contacting us directly. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
